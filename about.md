# About

I'm Tingxuan — a graduate student working somewhere between computational chemistry, machine learning, and the parts of the brain that nobody has a clean model for yet.

This site is a small, quiet corner of the internet where I keep two kinds of writing:

- **Essays** — personal and reflective. The pieces I write when I want to think about something slowly.
- **Notes** — more technical, often half-finished. Method walkthroughs, debugging logs, and the kind of small write-ups I wish more people published.

Both live here because I needed somewhere that wasn't a notebook, wasn't a tweet, and wasn't a paper.

If you want to get in touch, the easiest way is through [GitHub]({{ site.github_url }}).
